About TheTool

TheTool

Version 1.3.0

All-in-One Productivity Tools

TheTool is your comprehensive productivity companion, bringing together essential tools in one convenient Chrome extension.

Features:

  • Calculator
  • Stopwatch with lap timing
  • Countdown timer with presets
  • Pomodoro timer with sound alerts
  • Quick-view calendar with ISO week numbers
  • Note-taking with auto-save
  • Multiple beautiful themes
  • Keyboard shortcuts support
